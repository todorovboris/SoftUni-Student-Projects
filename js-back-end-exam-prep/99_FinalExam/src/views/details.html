2004 Indian Ocean Earthquake and Tsunami

Location: Indian Ocean, including Indonesia, Thailand, Sri Lanka, India, and others.

Type: Earthquake

Year of the Event: 2004

Interested: 2

A massive undersea earthquake with a magnitude of 9.1-9.3 struck off the west coast of northern Sumatra, Indonesia, triggering a series of devastating tsunamis. Cause (if known): Sudden rupture along the fault between the Burma Plate and the Indian Plate. Duration of the Event: The earthquake lasted approximately 8-10 minutes; tsunamis followed shortly after.
